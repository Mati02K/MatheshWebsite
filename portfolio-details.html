MEDIHUB

Project information

  • Category: AI Chatbot embedded in a App
  • Organizer: Smart India Hackathon Finals
  • Project start date: 01 March, 2020
  • Project URL: MediHub Info

MediHub is an Artificial Intelligence Chatbot used for delivering Health Information to the public. It is a project made for the Smart India Hackathon along with my other six team mates. Through MediHub a user can store his/her Medical Information, Check their BMI / BMR / Calories, Ask symptoms of various diseases to the chatbot, Get to know aboot by various medicines, Get nearby Hospitals and Pharmacy or contact a Online Doctor in case of Emergency, Get latest Health News, Set your Medication and sleep timing, Step Count just by interacting with a chatbot.
